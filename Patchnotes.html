Patch Notes

Full update history for Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA MegaMix+ (PC / Steam). Click any version to expand its changelog.

v 1.0.0 May 26, 2022
Initial Release Full Content
▼

New Content

  • 238 songs from the Arcade Future Tone library — the complete catalog on PC for the first time.
  • Dual gameplay modes: classic Arcade Mix (button-press) and MegaMix Mode (touch-influenced tap/swipe).
  • Full module (costume) system with 300+ unlockable outfits per character.
  • Steam Achievement integration with 62 unique achievements.
  • Keyboard, controller, and mouse input support at launch.
  • 4K resolution support and uncapped frame rate on PC.

Known Issues at Launch

  • Occasional audio desync reported at sample rates above 48 kHz.
  • Some AMD GPU configurations experienced shader compilation stalls on first run.

🎮 MegaMix+ marks the first time the full Project DIVA arcade song library has been available on a PC platform.

v 1.1.0 June 15, 2022
Bug Fix Performance QOL
▼

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed audio desync affecting players with system audio sample rates set to 96 kHz or above.
  • Resolved a crash that occurred when rapidly switching modules in the customisation menu.
  • Fixed incorrect note timing display on "The Disappearance of Hatsune Miku" at Extreme difficulty.
  • Corrected a rendering artefact on character hair in certain lighting stages.

Performance

  • Reduced shader precompilation time on first launch by approximately 35% for AMD GPUs.
  • Lowered VRAM footprint by ~180 MB in high-density crowd scenes.
  • Improved frame pacing on variable refresh rate (VRR / G-Sync / FreeSync) monitors.

Quality of Life

  • Added full key rebinding support for all gameplay inputs.
  • Note speed can now be adjusted in increments of 0.5× instead of 1× for finer control.
  • Song search now supports searching by artist/producer name in addition to song title.
v 1.2.0 October 4, 2022
DLC New Content Bug Fix
▼

DLC Content Added

  • Song Pack Vol. 1 available: 10 additional tracks including fan-voted selections.
  • Seasonal costume set "Autumn Harvest" — 6 new modules across all characters.
  • New DIVA Room furniture items: "Arcade Cabinet" and "Holographic Stage" sets.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed a progression blocker preventing some players from unlocking the "Perfect Chain" achievement.
  • Resolved memory leak causing performance degradation after 3+ hours of continuous play.
  • Corrected subtitle timing on 7 songs in the English localisation.
v 1.3.0 February 9, 2023
Accessibility Performance
▼

Accessibility

  • Added customisable note and hold colour schemes to assist players with colour vision deficiencies.
  • New "Safe Zone" setting reduces note density in the centre of the screen to ease target tracking.
  • UI scale option now ranges from 80% to 140% for high-DPI and accessibility needs.
  • Added screen reader support for all main menu elements.

Performance

  • Improved Steam Deck compatibility: stable 60 fps at medium settings in balanced power mode.
  • Added DLSS 2.4 and AMD FSR 2.0 upscaling support for lower-end GPUs.
  • Background asset streaming optimised to reduce hitches during song transitions.
v 1.4.0 August 31, 2023
Anniversary DLC QOL
▼

Miku's 16th Anniversary Update

  • Special "16th Anniversary" title screen and menu theme active through September 2023.
  • Commemorative module set: "Miku Birthday 2023" — limited-time unlock via in-game challenge.
  • Song Pack Vol. 2: 8 tracks added, including two newly arranged classics exclusive to PC.
  • New "Replay Mode" — save and replay your best runs frame-by-frame with adjustable speed.

Quality of Life

  • Score leaderboards now display friends' scores separately from the global ranking.
  • Added a "Practice Mode" for individual chart sections with adjustable loop points.
  • Song favourites list expanded from 50 to 200 entries.

♪ August 31 marks the anniversary of Hatsune Miku's original software release in 2007.

Winter Content
