* Songs in the game Project DIVA *

And here are some very famous songs from Project DIVA:

1. "ワールドイズマイン" (World is Mine) by ryo (supercell) - This song is one of the most iconic tracks in the Project DIVA series, featuring Hatsune Miku as the main vocalist. It has a catchy melody and playful lyrics that have made it a fan favorite.
2. "Tell Your World" by livetune - This song is another popular track in the Project DIVA series, known for its uplifting and energetic vibe. It features Hatsune Miku's vocals and has been widely praised for its catchy tune and positive message.
3. "千本桜" (Senbonzakura) by Kurousa-P - This song is a fast-paced and energetic track that has become a staple in the Project DIVA series. It features Hatsune Miku's vocals and has a unique blend of traditional Japanese instruments with modern electronic music.
4. "初音ミクの消失" (The Disappearance of Hatsune Miku) by cosMo@Bousou-P - This song is known for its fast tempo and complex rhythm, making it a challenging track in the Project DIVA series. It features Hatsune Miku's vocals and has a dramatic and intense atmosphere.
5. "メルト" (Melt) by ryo (supercell) - This song is a slower, more emotional track that has become a fan favorite in the Project DIVA series. It features Hatsune Miku's vocals and has heartfelt lyrics about love and longing.
