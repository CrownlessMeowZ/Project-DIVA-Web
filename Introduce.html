About Project DIVA

Project DIVA is a critically acclaimed series of rhythm games co-developed by SEGA and Crypton Future Media. Players tap and hold notes in sync with iconic Vocaloid tracks, while unlocking hundreds of costumes and exploring richly animated music videos featuring Hatsune Miku and her fellow Vocaloid characters.

Vocaloid Characters

Hatsune Miku

CV01 — The world's most recognizable virtual singer. Developed by Crypton Future Media, Miku's 16-year-old persona and signature teal twin-tails have become a global icon of digital pop culture.

Kagamine Rin

CV02 — The energetic and spirited twin sister of Len. Rin's bright yellow theme and rebellious personality shine across a diverse catalog of rock, electronic, and folk-style tracks.

Kagamine Len

CV02 — Rin's twin brother with a lighter, more youthful vocal tone. Len's songs often explore themes of adventure and emotion, ranging from high-energy electro to poignant ballads.

Megurine Luka

CV03 — A bilingual Vocaloid capable of singing in both Japanese and English. Luka's mature, cool-toned voice makes her the go-to choice for sophisticated, atmospheric compositions.

KAITO

The first Japanese male Vocaloid released for the Vocaloid 1 engine. KAITO's deep, warm baritone voice and blue scarf have made him a beloved figure since his 2006 debut.

MEIKO

The first Japanese female Vocaloid released for the Vocaloid 1 engine. MEIKO's powerful, expressive voice and bold red design set the foundation for the entire Crypton Vocaloid lineup.
