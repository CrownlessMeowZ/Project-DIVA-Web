* Information about Project DIVA *

Project DIVA is a series of rhythm games developed by Sega and Crypton Future Media, featuring the virtual singer Hatsune Miku and other Vocaloid characters. The games typically involve players pressing buttons in time with music to score points, and they often include a variety of songs and character customization options.

* Vocaloid Characters In Project DIVA *

1. Hatsune Miku, officially code-named CV01, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media.

2. Kagamine Rin, officially code-named CV02, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media.

3. Kagamine Len, officially code-named CV02, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media.

4. Megurine Luka, officially code-named CV03, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media.

5. Kaito is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media, officially recognized as the first Japanese male voicebank released for the Vocaloid 1 engine.

6. Meiko is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media, officially recognized as the first Japanese female voicebank released for the Vocaloid 1 engine.
